Overview

Purpose

Being in the Jackson Hole area, there is a lot of beautiful landscape to be experienced. We are one of many Whitewater Rafting companies in the area. Our main goal is that we want people that are from out of town to become familiar with us, and understand that their experience with us will be the best.

Audience

Our audience would be out of town people young people mostly looking for a thrill, and they will likely be young professionals. Accesing the website will provide a means to become familiar how to book and participate in our adventures. Their needs arent being met by our competetors and ourself at this time. They are having a hard time finding the information for meeting places and cost. This information through our site is typically accesed before their trip on their laptop, otherwise in town on their mobile phone.
